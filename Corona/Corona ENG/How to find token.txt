How to get tokens:

1- Enter discord in your browser and log in to your account.
2- Press Ctrl+Shift+I keys at the same time.
3- Press the number 1 shown in the picture named "1 & 2".
3- Press the place 2 shown in the picture named "1 & 2" and press "application".
4- Press the place shown in the picture named "2. picture"
5- Press the place shown in the red "filter", type token and press enter.
6- What is opposite the token text shown in the photo named "token" is your token. You can copy it by double clicking and ctrl+c.

NOTE: IF YOU CAN'T, THERE ARE DETAILED EXPLANATIONS ON YOUTUBE. YOU CAN FIND HOW TO FIND OR BUY TOKEN ON YOUTUBE.
